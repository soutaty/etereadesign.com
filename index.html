[ Currently based in Cologne ]

It's not only a design studio, it’s a way of “work-love-life” since 2008, when I decided I’d like to work with all kind of people.

I always wanted to keep learning and help people so they can benefit from it, whoever comes my way! We can profit from each other as we are all different to some degree.

Freedom to create is fundamental in my process. I develop all projects from different parts of the world width the help of my laptop and home office that I carry with my backpack. Eterea Design has been to Italy, China, Hong Kong, United States, Vienna, Germany, and is always searching for new places to create something and find inspiration.

Work is mostly done from my studio based in Cologne, Germany, but I am flexible as to time and place for a project anywhere in this world and as a self-employed graphic designer, I work for both brands and agencies. I am available for short-term and long-term projects, co-located anywhere in the world or remote.
