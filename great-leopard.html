Great Leopard is the first Kung Fu school I designed for, in partnership with Kung Fu teacher Keith Rios, after seeking inspiration and research from a traditional Kung Fu school in China.

Visual identity was developed for Nan Tien Men, illustrations project in partnership with Keith Rios and graphic design for classes, workshops, uniforms, stationary and certificates.
